About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at MIT Academy of Engineering (MIT-AOE), Pune. My Ph.D. research focuses on automated lung disease classification using machine learning on chest radiographs. I have over 5 years of teaching experience and have published in several reputed journals and conferences.
